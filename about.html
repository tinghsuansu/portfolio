About Me

I'm a Product Designer with 3+ years of UX experience and a master's in HCI from Carnegie Mellon University. My path started in industrial design, moved through visual communication and web design, and eventually landed in UX. That cross-disciplinary background shapes how I approach problems: I think about design holistically, across screens and physical space. I believe great UX enhances the way people interact with the world around them.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me drawing, making crafts, or playing piano and guitar. Anything that lets me build something with my hands.

I work well in...

Complex technical constraints:

I find challenges like hardware limitations, HMI systems, and IoT architectures often make the design problem more interesting.

Cross-functional teams:

I've learned to spot gaps between teams early and create shared documentations, flows, diagrams to get everyone on the same page before it becomes a problem.

Ambiguous, fast-moving projects:

I stay steady when things shift mid-project. People have told me I bring calm to chaotic situations and help a team move from confusion to clarity.
